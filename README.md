 What Is Afriflow AI?
Afriflow AI is a production-ready AI automation platform I designed, built, and commercially
deployed from scratch under my company, Bohlale Automated Services. It is actively
marketed and sold to small businesses across South Africa.
The platform automates the four biggest pain points for small businesses — customer
communication, appointment booking, lead tracking, and follow-up emails — using AI, so
business owners never miss a customer again.

How the Automation Works
1. Client pays on the checkout page via PayFast
2. Payment confirmed — funds deposited directly to owner’s bank account
3. Owner contacts client within 24 hours for a 30-minute onboarding call
4. AI is trained on the client’s business data — services, pricing, FAQs, and tone
5. WhatsApp bot, booking system, CRM, and email sequences go live within 48 hours
6. Monthly retainer collected automatically — owner monitors via admin dashboard
