FAQ

Questions answered

Do I need a verified WhatsApp Business account?
+
Yes — you need a WhatsApp Business account and we assist with the Meta Business API verification process as part of onboarding. It typically takes 2–5 business days to verify.
How long does setup take?
+
For Starter and Growth packages, the full system is live within 48 hours of completing the onboarding call. Enterprise setups with custom integrations take up to 7 business days.
Will the AI sound robotic to my customers?
+
No. We train the AI specifically on your business tone, language, and common phrases. We support Zulu, Sotho, and Afrikaans alongside English. Customers rarely realise they're talking to an AI.
What happens if the AI can't answer a question?
+
The AI gracefully hands off to you with a message like "Let me get a human to assist you with that" — and flags the conversation for follow-up. No dropped balls.
Is there a contract?
+
We operate on a month-to-month basis with a 30-day notice period. No lock-in — we earn your loyalty through results, not contracts.
What industries work best?
+
Beauty & hair salons, gyms, restaurants, clinics, estate agents, law firms, logistics companies, tutoring services, and any business that gets enquiries via WhatsApp. If you have customers, this works.
